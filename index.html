The Ideation Process

Manifest mentors and staff engage with entreprenuers on concepts that solve pressing cybersecurity concerns. Leaing on our experience and skills, we guide the team to focus on MVP, extreme customer engagement and rapid iteration on key features.

Relentless customer engagement

Manifest mentors and staff introduce entreprenuers to releveant customers and partners early in the ideation phase and help to form lasting relationships through the development and early adoption phases

Product and team evolution

Mentors advise on modern product development tools and efforts, allowing teams to sidestep common traps and pitfalls. The deep Manifest professional community provides access to key talent and advice, as the company grows.

Feeding the beast

Our experience tells us that properly managing the funding process, including the timing and levels, can easily make or break a young company. Our mentors have decades of experience in growing small companies and include many investors who can help to guide entrepeuers through the many pitfalls.

Building amazing cybersecurity companies - in Austin, Texas

The Manifest team is joined by a large group of Austin area cybersecurity veterans who believe that the Austin area has the perfect mix of talent, infrastructure, funding and support to become a premier cybersecurity hub. Manifest was founded to bring this community together to support these young innovators to avoid common missteps and dramatically increase their chances of success.
